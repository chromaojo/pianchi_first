Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

The only thing you need to do is to register with the institution and then you will be given an account, log in, and enroll for courses using your dashboard..

Will I get a certificate?

Yes, you’ll receive a certificate upon successful course completion.

Are the courses beginner-friendly?

Absolutely. Our programs are structured for both beginners and advanced learners.

What is the mode of learning?

We offer both online and on-site classes, depending on your preference.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes, installment options are available for select programs. You need to contact the admin or customer care department to be sure your selected course has that feature.
